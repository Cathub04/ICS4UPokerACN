Fishing kitten

Rule:each player get 1 deck of poker, take turn to reveal one card on their own deck of card, then put it on the table to form a list of card. When there's two same-number poker on list after a player put a card, that player can get all the card between those two same number card(include those two). If the card is "J", then player can grab all the card on list. The player run out of card first loses.
